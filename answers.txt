Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We might consider using Git if we want to collaborate with other developers on a project but we each want to make changes on our own laptops.
Additionally, Git lets you track changes made to files over time, which can then allow to revert to those changes easily.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to a variety of diferent fields. One example of is it being applied to the healthcare industry during a pandemic. Healthcare professionals could analyze a large amount of patient data to determine when/where outbreaks may occur based on sickness and patient trends.
